# On-Brand, editable diagrams with AI

Stakeholders often send us rough sketches or LLM-generated diagrams that take significant time to clean up and bring on-brand. I wanted one pipeline that turns any of these into on-brand, editable assets without redrawing from scratch.

I built a tokenized diagram generator that emits both editable draw.io files and Illustrator-safe SVGs from a shared design system, with a shape library and batch style-sync. It draws on Isaac Kim's brand icon library and auto-picks the right icon per concept — a big time saver. The result: faster turnaround, consistent typography, icons and connectors, and draw.io files stakeholders can keep iterating on.
